When to go on a trip?

Don't worry about anything, there is bound to be a destination that meets your expectations when you book a cheap last minute trip. You will only have to take into account the season you want to go to. Be aware that in summer, a stay in Asia will be dependent on the monsoon, the rainy season, which can sometimes be very violent. Winter is the ideal season to go to the southern hemisphere as the seasons are reversed, our winter is their summer. Conversely, northern destinations hardly see the light of day in winter. Iceland, Norway or Lapland are magical in any season, but unless you are chasing the northern lights, visible in winter, prefer to visit them in summer. Closer to Europe, there are many destinations where you can find the sun most of the year. Some Greek islands, the Turkish coast, Cyprus or the Canaries enjoy a pleasant climate all year round, and if you cannot be promised that you will bathe on the 1st of the year, be certain that the sun will accompany your days. The best seasons to go on a trip, both in terms of budget and the level of tranquility that you can hope for, are spring and autumn. In general, choose when to go, when other tourists do not rush to a destination by being well informed. You will have no trouble experiencing the end of the monsoon in Sri Lanka in October, as the rains are scarce and mostly nocturnal. In a nutshell, don't do like everyone else, be smart by looking for destinations you never thought of.
